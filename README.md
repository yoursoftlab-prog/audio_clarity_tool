# Audio_Clarity_Tool
Audio Clarity Tool is designed to make professional-quality audio enhancement simple, fast, and accessible for everyone.

**Audio Clarity Tool** is a fast, privacy-focused web application that enhances voice recordings directly in your browser. It removes background noise, reduces hiss, and improves speech clarity without uploading your audio to a server.

Whether you're a content creator, student, teacher, podcaster, journalist, or professional, Audio Clarity Tool helps you produce cleaner and more professional-sounding audio with just a few clicks.

## Features

* 🎙️ AI-powered voice enhancement
* 🔇 Background noise reduction
* 🎧 Hiss and hum removal
* ✨ Voice clarity enhancement
* 🔊 Automatic volume optimization
* 🎚️ Adjustable enhancement controls
* 🌐 Runs entirely in your browser
* 🔒 Privacy-first — your audio files never leave your device
* ⚡ Fast processing with no software installation
* 📁 Supports MP3, WAV, OGG, M4A, and FLAC audio formats

## Privacy

Your audio is processed locally in your browser whenever possible. We do not permanently store your audio recordings on our servers. Only account information and usage data required for authentication, free-trial tracking, and premium access are stored securely using Firebase.
